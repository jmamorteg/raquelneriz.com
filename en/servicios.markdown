# Practice Areas

These are Raquel Neriz's main lines of practice and accompaniment.

## Conservation and restoration {#restoration-conservation}

Restoration processes with an artisanal, aesthetic and symbolic perspective.

## Intentional tattooing {#intentional-tattoo}

Tattooing as a process of meaning and personal transformation, including oncological tattoo work.

## Restorative yoga {#restorative-yoga}

Body care and regulation practices adapted to each life moment.

## Plants and nature {#plants-nature}

Creative and therapeutic spaces inspired by botany, observation and connection to nature.
